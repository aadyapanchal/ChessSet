Download each file. If you have a 3D Printer, use it and 3rd-party software to print the chess set.

On Onshape, you can slide the Chess Board cover to open and close the half chess board to store and take out your chess pieces. 



The idea was inspired by Coca-Cola’s "Friendly Twist" campaign. You can have Coca-Cola bottles for free at the college campus, but to open them, you must have two bottles twisted on top of each other. This way, new college freshmen made many friends when starting on campus.
